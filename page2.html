Rid the Coasts of Clutter!

Interested in coastal cleanups?

We have various locations where we regularly operate and offer opportunities for those who are willing to participate in coastal cleanups whenever they want.

Become a volunteer today and join our thriving community of like-minded, passionate individuals who also wish to beautify these valuable coasts!

We are accepting anyone as long as they have the drive for taking action! Whether or not it's your first time volunteering, you can rest assured that this experience will be valuable. This is an opportunity for you to contribute to your local community while at the same time make new connections!

If you are interested, check out our available locations below and select the date and time you wish to join us! After which, you will be added to our list of participants for that specific date and timeframe.
