SOW THE SEEDS FOR A GREENER FUTURE!

Revive the greenery in your community!

Trees help make a place look appealing, and allows for cleaner oxygen to stick around. But you already knew this.

However, did you know that spending time around trees also reduces stress, and moreover, that these things provide a home for various little creatures, and help the surrounding ecosystem thrive?

Become a volunteer today, and you can help sow the seeds for a cleaner, greener future not just for you, but for your community, and all the little lives that rely on these giant living chunks of wood!

We are accepting anyone as long as they have the drive for taking action! Whether or not it's your first time volunteering, you can rest assured that this experience will be valuable. This is an opportunity for you to contribute to your local community while at the same time make new connections!

If you are interested, check out our available locations below and select the date and time you wish to join us! After which, you will be added to our list of participants for that specific date and timeframe.
