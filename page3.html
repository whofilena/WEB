Make the walk home for someone a little better.

Soda cans littered, white plastic bags being picked up by small winds, cigarette butts flattened on the ground. All of these sights make the walk home from work or from school, places that already induce much stress, even more stressful and unappealing!

What's this got to do with being a Steward of Nature, you ask? Dirty sidewalks don’t just look bad—they clog drains, pollute waterways, and harm local ecosystems through runoff and decay!

Why not get involved in our efforts to help prevent these ecosystems from being damaged any further, while at the same time making the trips back home just a bit better for those who walk these streets?

If you are interested, check out our available locations below and select the date and time you wish to join us! After which, you will be added to our list of participants for that specific date and timeframe.
